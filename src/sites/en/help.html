NeoLine Help Contents

Thank you for using NeoLine wallet, and if you have problems using it, please browse the following:

Wallet management

  • How to create wallet

    On the [wallet] homepage, click the drop-down button in the top bar to bring up the wallet list box and choose to create wallet. NeoLine supports multi-wallet digital asset management.

  • How to import wallet

    Currently, the private key, Encrypted Key and NEP2 are supported to import the wallet.

  • How to delete wallet

    In the wallet list box, select the corresponding wallet and hold to delete it.

Functional problems

  • What are the functions of [Wallet], [Browse] and [Setting] respectively?

    1. [Wallet]: You can select nodes, scan code to transfer, switch wallets and add assets.

    2. [Browse] : You can view details of each transaction and Information related to the blockchain, and access to some areas may be Limited.

    3. [Setting]: You can set up language, currency unit and node, also can see help contents and information about us.

  • How to view wallet information

    On the [wallet] homepage, click on the wallet avatar and enter the wallet details page to view the QR code, address and private key of the corresponding wallet.

  • How to add assets

    On the [wallet] homepage, click the button of adding asset and select the corresponding asset.

  • How to trade assets

    On the [wallet] homepage, click on the corresponding asset and enter the asset trading page. On this page, you can have transfers or receipts, and view the transaction record.

  • Why has the status of transfer been in the “to be confirmed”

    1. Please wait a moment, blockchain transfer can not be received immediately. Please be patient and you usually need to wait 30 seconds .

    2. If it is to be confirmed for more than 3 minutes, it may be caused by a node failure. The record will be deleted locally, don't worry!

safe issues

  • Forgot your password, what should I do?

    Password retrieval and modification are currently not supported. You can log in to the wallet with your private key.

  • What if I forget the private key?

    Be sure to keep your private key, and if you lose it, you will lose the assets in the wallet forever!

  • What should I do with the transfer error?

    Based on the "irrevocable" attribute of the blockchain operation, when you use the transfer function, please confirm the transfer address and amount, If an error occurs, the transaction cannot be cancelled.
